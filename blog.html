Getting Started with Our Platform

This practical guide explains how to evaluate and procure dedicated internet access (DIA) from Frontier, CenturyLink, and Lumen. It clarifies what DIA really means—guaranteed, symmetrical, SLA-backed bandwidth—and when it’s essential for mission‑critical sites. The post compares single‑carrier vs multi‑carrier strategies, outlines resilient designs with dual paths and SD‑WAN, and shows how to leverage free install promotions while avoiding over‑provisioned bandwidth. Finally, it details a repeatable, carrier‑neutral workflow for multi‑site projects and argues that, when designed strategically, DIA becomes a competitive advantage rather than just another telecom expense.
